About Spice Haven Exports

Spice Haven Exports is a small-scale, quality-driven enterprise dedicated to bringing the finest pepper, cardamom, coffee, and select spices from the pristine Western Ghats of Idukki, Kerala to global markets.

Rooted in tradition and guided by sustainability, we work closely with local farmers to ensure every product reflects the rich soil, pure climate, and timeless heritage of Idukki—often called God's Own Spice Garden.

Our Vision

To make Idukki's world-class spices accessible globally while preserving the environment, empowering farmers, and celebrating Kerala's spice heritage.

Our Values

Uncompromised Quality

Only carefully handpicked, naturally grown spices—free from adulteration and unnecessary processing. We believe in delivering excellence in every shipment.

Pure & Authentic

Our spices retain their natural oils, aroma, and flavor, just as nature intended. No artificial additives, just pure goodness from the Western Ghats.

Sustainably Sourced

We support eco-friendly farming practices and fair partnerships with small farmers in Idukki. Sustainability is at the heart of everything we do.

Fresh from the Source

Direct sourcing ensures maximum freshness, better traceability, and superior taste. We control quality from farm to export.

Our Signature Products

Handpicked from the fertile hills of Idukki, each product embodies the purity and richness of Kerala's spice heritage.

Idukki Black Pepper

Idukki Gold

Bold, aromatic, and rich in flavor. Our black pepper is sourced from the finest plantations in Idukki, where the unique climate and soil conditions create the perfect environment for growing premium quality pepper.

  • High piperine content
  • Strong aroma and bold flavor
  • Carefully sun-dried
  • Available in whole and ground forms

Green & Dried Cardamom

Queen of Spices

Naturally sweet, fragrant, and premium grade. Our cardamom pods are harvested at peak maturity and carefully processed to preserve their distinctive aroma and flavor profile.

  • High essential oil content
  • Intense natural fragrance
  • Bold green color
  • Export quality grading

Idukki Coffee

High Altitude Grown

Smooth, earthy, and grown at high altitudes. Our coffee beans benefit from the cool mountain climate and rich volcanic soil, creating a unique flavor profile that coffee lovers appreciate.

  • Grown above 3000 feet elevation
  • Shade-grown beans
  • Smooth, balanced flavor
  • Low acidity
